🚀 GitHub Pages Setup

One-time setup · Takes about 10 minutes · Free forever
Once done, your customers get a simple link. It opens in Safari, works offline after the first visit, and installs to the Home Screen in 2 taps. No files, no apps needed.

1
Create a free GitHub account
Go to github.com and sign up. Use any email — it's completely free.
2
Create a new repository
Click the + icon (top right) → New repository. Name it adhd-manager. Tick "Add a README file". Click Create repository.
3
Upload all your files
Click Add file → Upload files. Drag all 8 files from the list above into the upload box. Scroll down and click Commit changes.
4
Turn on GitHub Pages
Click Settings (top tabs) → scroll to Pages (left sidebar) → under "Branch" select main → click Save. Wait about 2 minutes.
5
Get your links
Go back to Settings → Pages. You'll see your URL — it looks like this:
https://YOURNAME.github.io/adhd-manager/adhd-dark-final.html
https://YOURNAME.github.io/adhd-manager/adhd-light-final.html
These are the links you give to customers.
6
Test on your iPhone
Open the link in Safari → tap the Share button (box with arrow) → tap "Add to Home Screen" → tap Add. Open it from your home screen — it runs full screen with no browser bar. Go to Airplane Mode and open it again — it still works. ✅
💡 Important: The icon files (icon-dark.png and icon-light.png) need to be 512×512 PNG images. You can create them for free at canva.com — just make a 512×512 design with a brain emoji or your product name, download as PNG, and rename the files.
